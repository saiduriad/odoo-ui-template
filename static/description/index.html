Overview

Employee assessment/training is the process by which an employee acquires the necessary skills, knowledge, behaviors, and contacts to effectively transition into a new organization.It can enhance the overall satisfaction of employees and can encourage a positive attitude about the employer. Employees view companies that offer meaningful benefits as more caring and engaged with their needs. This may help reduce turnover and increase productivity.
